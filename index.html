Frequently Asked Questions


Who are we, and what is Colupon for?

Colupon was made by four Columbia students who want to take full advantage of the fact that our university is based in New York City. We also happen to love saving money. Colupon is an easy way to find the establishments near you, where you can use your Columbia ID to save big money. In a few clicks, you can find great deals on fun outings, tasty food, and trendy shopping near you.

How do you redeem the listed discounts and deals?

We only include deals that require one criteria to redeem: a valid current Columbia ID. Simply go to any of the establishments listed, and show your student ID to them — and the discount will be yours.

Can you view directions to your chosen place?

When you click on a location, a pop-up will show you more information and details about that place. You’ll see an embedded Google Map, showing you the address and its distance from your specified location. To get turn-by-turn directions, walking directions, or public transit, simply click “more options” and you will be brought to a pre-filled Google Map.

Can you save places for later?

Yes! On a place’s page, all you need to do is click the “Add to Saved” button — this will add that place to your list of saved locations, which you can view on the Colupon homepage at any time.

Have a deal you’d like to add to our selection?

We would happily welcome your deal submission! If you know of an establishment that gives deals to Columbia students, please submit it to us and we’ll verify and add the discount to Colupon. On the Colupon homepage at the bottom, there is a form to submit your deal.
