Help and Support


What is this application?

New York City provides all kinds of discounted deals for Columbia students. However, Columbia students still stubled through finding activities and places to visit. Having limited amount of money to spend and time to do area search, Columpon developed a clean and easy to use platform for students to search for great deals.

How I get the deals to these places?

Click on the awesome places you want to go, follow the direction on the map and remember to bring your Columbia Student ID. When you arrived at the place, simply show your student ID and you will be all set.

Can I save places I want to visit?

Once you find places, follow the direction.

How do I add a deal?

We encourage students continue sharing good deals with us. You will find a section in the homepage to sumbit us a deal you find in New York.

How can I contact Colupon?

You are welcome to email us anytime: ask@colupon.com
